\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Hookean linear elastic material in 3D. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classfeatk_isotropic_linear_elastic3_d_material}{featk\+Isotropic\+Linear\+Elastic3\+D\+Material}} is a 3D linear elastic \mbox{\hyperlink{classfeatk_material_base}{featk\+Material\+Base}} governed by Hooke\textquotesingle{}s law. \mbox{\hyperlink{classfeatk_isotropic_linear_elastic3_d_material}{featk\+Isotropic\+Linear\+Elastic3\+D\+Material}} generates and stores the non-\/symmetrized 4th-\/order Hooke\textquotesingle{}s tensor (i.\+e. a Eigen\+::\+Matrix$<$double, 9, 9$>$), given its Young\textquotesingle{}s modulus $E$ and Poisson\textquotesingle{}s ratio $\nu$. For generalization purpose of the solving routines, the Hooke\textquotesingle{}s law is not explicitly symmetrized in featk, hence Hooke\textquotesingle{}s tensor is re-\/arranged as follows\+:

\[ \begin{bmatrix} \sigma_{xx}\\ \sigma_{xy}\\ \sigma_{xz}\\ \sigma_{yx}\\ \sigma_{yy}\\ \sigma_{yz}\\ \sigma_{zx}\\ \sigma_{zy}\\ \sigma_{zz}\\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2\mu+\lambda & 0 & 0 & 0 & \lambda & 0 & 0 & 0 & \lambda\\ 0 & \mu & 0 & \mu & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & \mu & 0 & 0 & 0 & \mu & 0 & 0\\ 0 & \mu & 0 & \mu & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\\ \lambda & 0 & 0 & 0 & 2\mu+\lambda & 0 & 0 & 0 & \lambda\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & \mu & 0 & \mu & 0\\ 0 & 0 & \mu & 0 & 0 & 0 & \mu & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & \mu & 0 & \mu & 0\\ \lambda & 0 & 0 & 0 & \lambda & 0 & 0 & 0 & 2\mu+\lambda \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} u_{x,x}\\ u_{x,y}\\ u_{x,z}\\ u_{y,x}\\ u_{y,y}\\ u_{y,z}\\ u_{z,x}\\ u_{z,y}\\ u_{z,z}\\ \end{bmatrix} \quad \lambda = \frac{\nu E}{(1+\nu)(1-2\nu)}, \quad \mu=\frac{E}{2(1+\nu)} \]
